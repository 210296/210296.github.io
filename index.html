Simple Origami

This is a paper crane

Origami cranes are one of the more easy origami figures to do. They are also pretty traditional.

Harder Origami

This is an origami turtle

Origami turtles are harder to make because it's more 3D and with all the patterns on its shell. (I can't make it.)

3D origami

This is an origami dragon

I personally find 3D origami a lot more amazing than the other type but making one is time consuming. They are also very hard (I can't make one.)

More origami

So what's this all about?

There are other types of origami such as modular origami. Modular origami is origami made up in multiple pieces. There are many figures ranging from easy to hard.

Origami can be quite entertaining one you get really into folding the figure you want. It can be a little frustrating when you can't make the folds right or get confused. If you're starting origami I suggest you start with something easier (like the paper crane or something even easier) then work from there. I may not be the best but I still find it quite enjoyable.
